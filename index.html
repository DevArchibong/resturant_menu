Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Vegetable Spring Rolls

    Rice paper rolls filled with crisp vegetables, herbs, and sometimes noodles, served with a tangy peanut or hoisin dipping sauce.

    Light and healthy, often served chilled—great for a fresh start to a meal.

  • Caprese Skewers

    Cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and basil leaves on skewers, drizzled with olive oil and balsamic reduction.

    A no-cook appetizer that’s colorful, refreshing, and easy to serve.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Chicken Tikka Masala

    Chunks of marinated chicken cooked in a creamy, spiced tomato-based sauce, served with basmati rice or naan bread.

  • Spaghetti Carbonara

    Italian pasta dish made with eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, and black pepper, tossed with spaghetti until creamy.

    Simple yet decadent, it’s a staple of Roman cuisine.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Baklava

    Layered phyllo pastry filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with honey or syrup, flavored with cinnamon or rosewater. Sweet, sticky, and packed with texture.

  • Lemon Tart

    Buttery tart crust filled with tangy lemon curd and topped with a light dusting of powdered sugar. Bright, zesty, and perfect for a refreshing end to a meal.
